# StockDatabase's Mission Statement
StockDatabase is founded by two SWE students, Philippe Christian Frøkjær Bourrachot and William Westergaard Jacobsen, in Aalborg, Denmark, while studying at Aalborg University (AAU).

StockDatabase is a data infrastructure company focused on providing accessible, structured, and affordable financial data for developers, analysts, and investers. The company was 
established in response to the high cost and limited accessibility of existing stock information APIs, particularly those offering historical financial reports. Recognizing a clear 
inefficiency in the market, the we set out to build a solution that removes paywall barriers and reduces cost without compromising data quality.

The core mission of StockDatabase is to reduce cost to access financial data by delivering reliable, normalized, and developer-friendly APIs that expose historical and current company 
fundamentals. The platform is designed to support use cases ranging from quantative analysis and algorithmic trading to educational tools and independent research.

StockDatabase emphasizes simplicity, transparency, and scalability. Data is structured for direct integration, minimizing preprocessing overhead and enabling rapid deployment in production
systems. The company prioritizes cost-efficiency to ensure that individuals, startups, and smaller institutions can access the same level of financial insight traditionally reserved for
large organizations.

By lowering the financial and technical barriers to high quality market data, StockDatabase positions itself foundational layer in the modern financial data ecosystem.
